1. The first period (1590-1600):
22 plays: 11 comedies, 3 tragedies and 8 historical plays. A period of youthfulness, optimism and rich imagination. E.g. Romeo and Juliet

2. The second Period (1601-1608):
A period of tragedies; Gloom and depression combined with masterly workmanship. E.g. Hamlet

3. The third period (1609-1612):
A period of dramatic romances. Unrealistic compromises and fantasy, restored serenity and tolerant resignation. E.g. The Tempest

At the time of his death, literary luminaries such as Ben Jonson hailed his works as timeless. “He was not of an age,but for all time.” --- Ben Jonson

He wrote 37 very successful plays, 154 sonnets
His vocabulary was enormous -somewhere between 17,000 and 34,000 words!
The estimated vocabulary of an educated person today is around 15,000 words.
